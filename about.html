About Me

Hi! I’m a self-taught artist passionate about expressing emotions and capturing what I love most through my drawings. My journey began in school, where winning two first prizes and a second prize in drawing competitions ignited my love for art. Over time, I practiced tirelessly, turning art into my passion and a core part of who I am.

Though I had to pause my artistic journey during higher studies, I’m now back, sharpening my skills and exploring new mediums like ink, charcoal, and painting, alongside my proficiency in pencil sketching.

Art, for me, is more than a skill—it’s a way to connect with myself and others. To share my journey and grow my network, I post my work on platforms like Instagram, Threads, and YouTube, aiming to reach a broader audience and inspire others.
